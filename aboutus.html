Our Story

The journey of family, flavor, and fire.

A Recipe Passed Down

Armed with decades of butchery experience and a hand full of recipes perfected in the back yard, Ron Meyer set out to open a local BBQ joint that reminded him of the no frills hole in the wall places he loved so much while traveling. Ron couldn't do it alone so he recruited his son-in-law and BBQ fanatic Josh Diaz as his business partner.

Over the past 20 years, Ron, Josh, and their family have made a successful team serving thousands of hungry BBQ lovers from every corner of the world. Our pit is always burning, and our doors are always open.

Our Commitment

At Piggy Blues BBQ, we're dedicated to serving authentic, slow-smoked barbecue with a side of true Minnesota hospitality. We believe in quality ingredients, a passion for flavor, and a commitment to our community.
